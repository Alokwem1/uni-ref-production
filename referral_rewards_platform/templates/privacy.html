Privacy Policy

At UNI-REF, your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect it.

1. Information We Collect
  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your username, email, and password (securely hashed).
  • Referral Data: We track who referred you and whom you referred to ensure rewards are accurate.
  • Payment Details: For cashout purposes, we collect your bank name and account number (not shared with third parties).
  • Ad Interaction: We may log your ad views and engagement to deliver rewards and improve content relevance.
2. How We Use Your Information
  • To verify your account and track referral rewards accurately.
  • To process your payments and cashout requests.
  • To prevent fraud, fake accounts, or system abuse.
  • To communicate with you about your activity, rewards, or platform updates.
3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard practices to protect your information. All passwords are encrypted, and sensitive data is handled with care. Only authorized personnel have access to user information.

4. Sharing of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third-party company or advertiser. Data is only used internally to power core platform features such as referrals, ad tracking, and rewards.

5. Cookies

We may use basic cookies to enhance user experience, remember sessions, and track referral links. You can disable cookies via your browser settings, but some features may stop working.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account at any time. To do so, simply log in to your dashboard or contact support if needed.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may occasionally update this privacy policy to reflect platform changes. All updates will be posted here, and major changes may be communicated via email or dashboard alert.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding your privacy on UNI-REF, contact us directly:
